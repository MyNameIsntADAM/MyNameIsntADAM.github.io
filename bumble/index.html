Why Bees?

The U.S. Department of Agriculture estimates that one-third of every human's diet is based off of honeybee dependent crops. Honey bees are currently disappearing at an alarming rate.

Our Mission

Bumble aims to replenish the threatened bee population while supporting both the community and croplands with honey and pollination services. The company educates consumers on the importance of bees, fostering a positive social, environmental, and economic influence in Kern County and beyond.
